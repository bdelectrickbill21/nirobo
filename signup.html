Nirobo Submission Rules

ALLOWED:

  • Educational content (courses, tutorials, research)
  • Cultural & historical content (photos, videos, documentaries)
  • News articles from reputable sources
  • Creative works with context (art, music, literature)
  • Cloud files with educational value (PDFs, docs, spreadsheets)
  • Social media posts with knowledge/informational value

NOT ALLOWED:

  • Hate speech, harassment, or personal attacks
  • Random/unrelated content (vlogs, selfies, daily life)
  • Spam, ads, phishing, or illegal content
  • Copyright infringement (pirated movies, songs, books)
  • Misinformation or fake news

PENALTIES:

  • Minor violation → Warning + removal
  • Moderate violation → 7-day ban
  • Severe violation → Permanent ban
